Satoru Iwata

1959 - 2015

Here's a timeline of Satoru Iwata, one of Nintendo's greatest people:

  • 1959 - Born 6th December in Sapporo, Japan

  • 1980 - Joined developers HAL Laboratory while attending university. HAL are famous for developing the Kirby games, amongst others

  • 1993 - Became the president of HAL to help achieve financial stability

  • 2002 - Became Nintendo's president and under his direction, Nintendo went on to develop the Nintendo DS and Wii

  • 2009 - Nintendo achieved record profits. Barron's (a weekly American magazine) placed Iwata in top 30 of worldwide CEO's

  • 2014 - In June 2014, a tumour was discoverd during a routine exam. This was removed and Satoru returned to work in October of that year

  • 2015 - The problem resurfaced and Iwata died on July 11th. He was awarded the Lifetime Achievement Award at the 2015 Golden Joystick Awards

    • Throughout Iwata's career, he became the face of Nintendo and regulary appeared in broadcasts including "Iwata Asks" and "Nintendo Directs."
